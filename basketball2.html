Basketball History:

Basketball was invented in December 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, as a less injury-prone indoor activity using a soccer ball and peach baskets. It was created to keep students active during the winter. Originally, there was no dribbling, only passing, and the first game ended 1-0. Eventually the shot clock was created to speed up the game and prevent a team from stalling. The NBA formed in 1949 and global popularity followed soon after. In the 1936 Olympics in Berlin, basketball was officially introduced as a sport.
