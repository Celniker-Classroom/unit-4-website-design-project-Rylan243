Basketball History:

Basketball was invented in December 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, as a less injury-prone indoor activity using a soccer ball and peach baskets. Dr. James Naismith was a physical education instructor at the International Young Men’s Christian Association Training School, which is now Springfield College. Its creation was met with great enthusiasm and it was made to keep students active during the winter. Originally, there was no dribbling, only passing, and the first game ended 1-0. Eventually the shot clock was created to speed up the game and prevent a team from stalling. The NBA formed in 1949 and global popularity followed soon after. In the 1936 Olympics in Berlin, basketball was officially introduced as a sport. Basketball also has been played around the globe, in places like Spain, Italy, China, and Australia, where many contributions have been made to its varied history.
