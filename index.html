What Basketball does for you:

Basketball is a highly beneficial sport that improves physical health, sharpens mental skills, and reduces stress. It also promotes essential life skills such as teamwork, discipline, and building social connections. Playing basketball strengthens muscles, increases flexibility, and enhances coordination. Overall, basketball is a great sport because it builds confidence by helping players overcome challenges.

Basktball History:

Basketball was invented in December 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, as a less injury-prone indoor activity using a soccer ball and peach baskets. It was created to keep students active during the winter. Originally, there was no dribbling, only passing, and the first game ended 1-0. Eventually the shot clock was created to speed up the game and prevent a team from stalling.
